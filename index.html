Interval Notation (for numbers A, B):

A:B (e.g. 5:4) - a frequency ratio

A#B (e.g. 4#12) - an equal tempered interval, A steps in B-EDO

A c (e.g. 100 c) - a value in cents

Determines how close (in cents) the combination of basis intervals must be to the goal interval.
